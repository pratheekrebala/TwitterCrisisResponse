Title: Disasters on social media
Description: Contributors looked at over 10,000 tweets culled with a variety of searches like “ablaze”, “quarantine”, and “pandemonium”, then noted whether the tweet referred to a disaster event (as opposed to a joke with the word or a movie review or something non-disastrous).
Provenance: https://www.crowdflower.com/data-for-everyone/
Added: September 4, 2015
Data Rows: 10877
Download Link: https://www.crowdflower.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/socialmedia-disaster-tweets-DFE.csv
